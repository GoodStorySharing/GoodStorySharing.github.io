I am a Ph.D. student majoring in Computer Science and Research Associate in State University of New York (SUNY) at Binghamton, USA since the fall semester 2019. I obtained my undergraduate and master degree from Chongqing University, China in 2016 and 2019, separately. My research interests are 1)Task and Motion Planning (TMP): the intersection of Planning from Artificial Intelligence and Motion Planning from Robotics (including vehicles), 2)Reinforcement Learning and 3)Autonomous Driving.

I am passionate about solving issues and creating unexplored values for people. I was the only Academic Star in 2018 at College of Computer Science at Chongqing University, as well as the only Academic Star in 2019 at BDSC group. I have several papers as the first author, including PerCom , TITS and ISJ. I am open to all kinds of academic communication. Please feel free to contact me via yding25@binghamton.edu.
